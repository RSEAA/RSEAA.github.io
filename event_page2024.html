Welcome to the RSE Asia Australia Unconference 2024 (RSEAA24) happening from September 10th to 13th! As we gear up for this exciting event, we want to share some important details to help you prepare.

Please read the information below ahead of the event so that you know what to expect and how to make the most of RSEAA24.

We would like to thank our Key Partners ARDC, AURIN, and the Australian Access Federation (AAF) for supporting us, as well as our Allied Partner, NCI Australia. We would also like to thank our Accessibility Partner, the Society of RSE, for their support.

Accessibility

We recommend that all participants install the Zoom client and make sure that it is updated to the latest version to successfully join the event.

Transcription and captioning will be provided using Red Bee Media for the welcome, keynotes, and the panel.

We are also delighted to announce that we have two Accessibility Fellows, Harsh Kalra and Sanchit Ghule.

They are here to help us understand what it is like to attend RSEAA24 as part of the blind and low vision community, and they will be writing a blog post about their experiences.

We know they will make suggestions to improve accessibility, and we will use this to reflect and ask how we can incorporate changes into RSEAA25.
